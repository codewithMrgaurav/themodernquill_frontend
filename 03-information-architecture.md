## 03 – Information Architecture & Routing

- **Goals**:
  - Define all pages, routes, and URL structure for SEO and usability.
- **Core routes**:
  - `/` – Home
  - `/category/[slug]` – Category listing (up to 30 categories)
  - `/blog/[slug]` – Post detail
  - `/search` – Search results
  - `/author/[slug]` – Author profile
  - `/login`, `/register`
  - `/admin` + nested routes for dashboard, editor, SEO, ads, media.
- **Deliverables**:
  - Site map diagram (can be done in Figma or a separate doc).
  - Confirmed route names and URL patterns.
